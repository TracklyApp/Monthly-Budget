👋 Welcome!

This app helps you manage your income, savings and expenses so you always know where your money goes. Everything is saved locally on your device — simple, fast, no account needed.

🌐 Recommended Browser

This app was built and tested in Google Chrome. Recommended browsers: Chrome or Edge on Windows, Chrome on Android, Safari on Mac, iPhone & iPad.

Other browsers may work, but some features (such as automatic backup file saving) might not be available.

💾Backup & restore your data

⚡Automatic Backup

Available in Chrome and Edge on desktop. Choose a folder once — your data saves automatically after every change.

No backup folder selected. Choose a backup location.
Your app data is stored locally in your browser on each device. Because browser storage is not permanent, we strongly recommend creating regular backups to keep your data safe.

To use the app across multiple devices, save your backups to Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud Drive. This makes importing and exporting between devices much faster.

💡 We recommend Google Drive — it works well across Windows, Mac, Android, and iPhone.

🖥️ Computer Setup

  1. Install Google Drive or OneDrive on your computer and sign in
  2. Wait for the Google Drive or OneDrive folder to appear in your file manager
  3. In the app, go to Backup → Automatic Backup → Choose Backup Location
  4. Select a folder inside your Google Drive or OneDrive
  5. Your backups will now save automatically and sync to your other devices

📱 Phone & Tablet Setup

  1. Install the same cloud storage app on your phone or tablet and sign in with the same account
  2. Open the app and find your backup folder
  3. Make the folder available offline so it syncs automatically
  4. In Simple Budget App, go to Backup → Import JSON Backup
  5. Select the latest backup file from your cloud folder
💡 Before switching devices, make sure your latest backup was saved on the device you used last.
